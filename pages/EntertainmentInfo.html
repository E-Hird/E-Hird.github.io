Benifits Of Computing In The Entertainment Industry

There many areas of entertainment industry where benifits of computing can be seen such as gaming, films and art.

Benifits in gaming

As computers have advanced over, digital games has made more available to the mass and the quality of games and speed at which they are being developed has increased. The video games industry has grown massively as games can be made quicker and in smaller development team for same quality years ago.

Benifits in films

Due to many advancements of computers, many parts of films have change a lot such as resolution and length of films, which improves audiences exprience when watching films. Resolution of films have improve a lot due to the improvement of computers being able to process more data and display more colours.

Benifits in music

The availability of music has increased dramatically due to the distrubution of music being easy. Also, music can be developed and edited with digital software, which can make more unique sounds than traditional means.

Benifits in art

The ability to veiw art have never been easier than ever and all you have to do is to look up online. Due to advancements in computers, art can be distrubed and create digitally, which allows the entertainment industry to create better entertainment.
