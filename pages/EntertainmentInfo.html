Exploring the Benefits of Computing
There many areas of entertainment industry where benefits of computing can be seen such as gaming, films and art.

Benefits in films

Due to many advancements of computers, many parts of films have change a lot such as resolution and length of films, which improves audiences experience when watching films. Resolution of films have improve a lot due to the improvement of computers being able to process more data and display more colours.

Benefits in music

The availability of music has increased dramatically due to the distribution of music being easy. Also, music can be developed and edited with digital software, which can make more unique sounds than traditional means.

Benefits in art

The ability to view art have never been easier than ever and all you have to do is to look up online. Due to advancements in computers, art can be distributed and create digitally, which allows the entertainment industry to create better entertainment.

Benefits in gaming

As computers have advanced over, digital games has made more available to the mass and the quality of games and speed at which they are being developed has increased. The video games industry has grown massively as games can be made quicker and in smaller development team for same quality years ago.
